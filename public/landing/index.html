Making it easier to work with data.
An online workbench with a secure three-step workflow that quickly positions your data for use.
Step 1
Catalogue Data
Securely connect to your data sources. Validate the data within each source. Check the relationships between different sources. Explore and transform the data to ensure it is fit for purpose. Answer questions relating to the content, quality and lineage of the data. Approve for use and proceed to step 2.  
Step 2
Contextualise Data
Quickly map the data from multiple sources to a standard set of integrated events. Rely on predefined templates to automatically calculate measures and to provide dimensions for exploring and verifying results. Answer questions relating to the metrics and their breakdown. Approve for use and proceed to step 3.  
Step 3
Consider & Distribute the Results
Visualise your integrated data and results immediately. Alternatively, package and distribute them for use in analytical, dash-boarding, machine learning and reporting tools; upload them to databases; share them on file services. Start using your data!  
Supported by a library of components that simplifies the sourcing, integration, and distribution of data.
